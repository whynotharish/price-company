Cookies

Clickfix uses “cookies” and similar tracking technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, flask cookies to enhance User experience, improve our Services, and provide personalized content and advertisements. A “cookie” is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit our Website. These cookies may be session-based (temporary) or persistent (stored for a longer duration). Our Website may also allow third-party services to place cookies on your device for analytics, advertising, or audience measurement. These third parties may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more details.

Types of Cookies:

  • Essential Cookies – Required for the proper functioning of our Website, including authentication, security, and maintaining user sessions.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies – Help us analyze User behavior, monitor website traffic, and improve service functionality.
  • Personalization Cookies – Store User preferences and enhance User experience by remembering login details, language preferences, and other custom settings.
  • Advertising & Marketing Cookies – Used to show advertisements, track engagement, measure advertising performance, and conduct audience analytics.
  • Web Beacons & Pixel Tags – Tiny graphics embedded in emails or web pages to track usage, engagement, and the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

How We use Cookies: Generally, Clickfix use cookies to identify User(s) and enable Clickfix to:

  • access User’s registration information or account information so User(s) do not have to re-enter it;
  • gather statistical information about usage by Users(s);
  • research visiting patterns and help target advertisements based on User(s) interests;
  • assist Clickfix’s partners to track User(s) visits to the Website; and
  • track progress and participation on the Website.

Managing Cookies: By using our Website with your browser settings set to accept cookies, you consent to our use of cookies and tracking technologies in the manner described above. Users can modify their browser settings to accept, block, or delete cookies. Please note that if you choose to delete or block your cookies, you may lose the ability to save certain preferences such as your username and password, and may be required to re-enter login credentials upon each visit.
